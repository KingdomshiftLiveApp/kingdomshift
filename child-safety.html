KingdomShift.Live Child Safety Standards

KingdomShift.Live is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Prohibited Content

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual exploitation of minors
  • Grooming or predatory behavior
  • Human trafficking involving minors
  • Any content that endangers children

Reporting and Enforcement

Users may report content, accounts, livestreams, comments, messages, or behavior that violates these standards.

Reported content may be reviewed, removed, and reported to appropriate authorities where required by law.

KingdomShift.Live reserves the right to suspend or permanently terminate accounts that violate these standards.
